== GmbH

     Which is written after the name of a German company, designates
	 is as a private entity.

	 THe letters stand for
	 	 Gesellschaft mit beschränkter Haftung,
	 means a 'company with limited liability'.

== AG
	 publicly traded

	 an abbreviation for the German word
	   Aktiengesellschaft,
	 which literally translates to 'stock corporation' or
	 	   'shares corporation' in English.
		   
== acontis
     完整符合 EtherCAT Master Classes指令 (ETG.1500)
	 协议栈 独立于 操作系统和编译器
	 支持多种CPU架构
	 支持SMP多核处理
	 提供两人个产品版本：
		Class A  ;;完整功能
		Class B  ;;基础功能
     支持从站之间通信
	 支持分布式时钟(DC)、模式同步（包括主站同步) (DCM)
